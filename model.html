Models(Varients)

The models which are there for the NISSAN GLORIA is has follows -

Y30 - This generation of the Nissan Gloria was luanched in 1983 and saw a major restyle of the previous generation. It had a box style shape with a straight-six engine, which had been used for many years, was upgraded to an all new V6-design, called the VG series engine

Y31 -The Y31 sedan was introduced in 1987 with the four-speed automatic transmission is now computer controlled for smoother shifts.

Y32 - This generation was introduced June 1991, and was offered as a sedan, The VG series engine continues to be offered with the 5-speed computer-controlled automatic transmission, with the 4-speed offered with the RD28 diesel engine. A manual transmission was no longer offered.

Y33 - The Y33 series Nissan Gloria is given a body restyle and introduced June 1995. Major changes were the introduction of the newly developed VQ series engine, and replacing the VG series, with the VQ30DET turbo utilizing an intercooler and DOHC valvetrain architecture

Y34 - June 1999 saw the release of the final version of the Gloria, with design assistance from Porsche. Direct Injection is introduced on all engines with the "DD" designation. The AWD was offered only on vehicles with the RB25DET engine. The top levels 300 ULTIMA-Z and the 300 ULTIMA-ZV were available with the CVT transmission as the only option.
